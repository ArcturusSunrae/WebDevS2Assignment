Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery

The Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery is a well-known conservation project and tourist attraction located in Hikkaduwa, a coastal town in Sri Lanka.The Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery has been operating for several years, contributing to sea turtle conservation efforts in Sri Lanka.

The Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery is dedicated to the conservation and protection of sea turtles and their eggs. The hatchery aims to ensure the survival and well-being of sea turtles by collecting and relocating eggs from vulnerable nesting sites to the hatchery, where they are incubated and later released into the ocean.

At the hatchery, the collected turtle eggs are carefully incubated under controlled conditions to maximize the hatching success rate. The staff ensures that the eggs receive the appropriate temperature and humidity levels required for their development.

Hikkaduwa offers a mesmerizing coastal view that captivates the hearts of all who visit. With its golden sands meeting the azure waters of the Indian Ocean, the view is nothing short of breathtaking. Palms sway in the gentle breeze, framing the coastline with their verdant fronds.

With its rustic charm and tranquil surroundings, the hatchery serves as a vital hub for the nurturing and rehabilitation of these ancient mariners. Visitors are greeted with the heartwarming sight of carefully tended turtle nests and hatchlings making their tentative journey to the sea.

Gathered around large tanks or enclosures, visitors are guided by knowledgeable staff as they offer specially prepared food to the turtles. The turtles, ranging from curious hatchlings to majestic adults, eagerly approach the food, creating an interactive and memorable encounter.

At the Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery, visitors are often invited to participate in the feeding sessions, where they can observe and even contribute to the well-being of the resident turtles.

Engaging in a variety of water sports in Hikkaduwa adds an exhilarating dimension to your coastal experience. The town's vibrant waters and steady breezes provide the perfect playground for thrill-seekers and water enthusiasts.

Snorkeling enthusiasts are treated to an underwater paradise of vibrant coral reefs and a kaleidoscope of marine life.
