Exploring
Turtles

Identification

Through careful observation of shell patterns, sizes, and coloration, we can differentiate between species like the loggerhead, green, hawksbill, and leatherback turtles. Our hatchery not only nurtures and releases these turtles into their natural habitats but also contributes valuable data to scientific research efforts. Join us in the fascinating journey of turtle identification and conservation.

Awareness

Our mission is to raise awareness about these gentle giants and their conservation needs. By learning about the various species that grace our oceans, such as the graceful loggerhead, the vibrant hawksbill, the majestic leatherback, and the serene green turtle, you can help protect their habitats and ensure their survival for generations to come. Join us in spreading the word.

Preservation

By collaborating with local communities, scientists, and conservationists, we strive to create a harmonious coexistence between humans and turtles. Through rehabilitation, research, and education, we work tirelessly to ensure that future generations can witness the awe-inspiring sight of turtles gracefully navigating the ocean currents. Join us in our journey to ensure the survival of these ancient beings and the preservation of the natural wonders that they call home.
